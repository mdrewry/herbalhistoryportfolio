Herbal History

Welcome to Herbal History's Portfolio.

Herbal History is a mobile e-journal app for (medical) marijuana users to record their cannabis usage sessions. Users can customize the journal to add sections and questionnaires based on their own personal reasons for using cannabis.

The Research

The Research

Contextual Inquiries

We performed contextual inquiries with a couple of cannabis users.
The contextual inquiries consisted of:
Background questions about their cannabis use
Working together to determine what kinds of elements they would want in a cannabis e-journal
Filling out an existing cannabis journal to observe their habits (made by Curaleaf)
Follow-up questions about the journal

We interviewed a few cannabis users about their cannabis habits, reasons for using cannabis, and their likeliness to use a cannabis journal.
Some things we asked about:
Their past experiences with health tracking apps Kinds of factors they would like to record if they were using a cannabis journal app
